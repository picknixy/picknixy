What Is The 29-Day Flat Stomach Formula?

The 29-Day Flat Stomach Formula is a comprehensive digital weight loss program created by certified personal trainer Derek Wahler.

Unlike traditional diets that focus on starvation, this program focuses on Metabolic Optimisation.

It argues that traditional cardio (like running on a treadmill for an hour) actually *increases* cortisol (stress hormone), which tells your body to store belly fat. This program uses short, specific workouts and nutritional tweaks to flip the switch from "Store Fat" to "Burn Fat."

"You can't out-train a bad hormonal environment. If your body thinks it's under attack, it will hold onto belly fat for survival. You need to signal safety to start burning fat."
